# Build Guide

## Preparation

### Materials
- Raspberry Pi 5
- NVMe base from Pimoroni

> #### Only applies for the Blueprint:
> - 4 mm plywood
> - Plexiglass
> - 4 wood screws 3×8 mm
> - 40×40 mm fan and fan grill
> - M2.5 washers

### Tools
- Wood glue
- Small flat-head and T10 screwdriver

> - Laser cutter with more than 1000 W power

## Steps
- Set up the Raspberry Pi and NVMe base, leaving 2 of the 4 screws out diagonally.
- Glue the box together, making sure all parts are oriented correctly.
- Screw the fan into the designated position.
- Insert the Raspberry kit and fasten it from below with the 2 remaining stand-off screws.
- Connect the fan pins.
- Attach the plexiglass panel using the wood screws.

> [!Note]
> The rubber feet of the NVMe base can remain attached during assembly.
